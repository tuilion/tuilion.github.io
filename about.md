**Tuilion** is a quiet blog about building tools, learning cybersecurity, and walking away from the performance theatre of academia.

I’m not here to teach, go viral, or build a “personal brand.” I write to think — to track what I build, what I break, and what I learn along the way.

I come from an academic background. Now I’m learning Python, exploring red teaming, and writing small, practical tools that solve real problems.

This blog is a logbook. If you're following a similar path — away from institutions and toward autonomy — maybe it helps.

### What You'll Find Here

- Logs from building and breaking things
- Python scripts, automation, checklists
- Notes on cybersecurity, Linux, and digital infrastructure
- Reflections from someone leaving the old world behind

Fragments at dawn. Thoughts at dusk.
